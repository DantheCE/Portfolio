I'm a systems engineer who operates across the full technical stack — from writing bare-metal firmware in C to designing distributed backend services that scale. My work sits at the intersection of low-level performance and high-level product thinking.

My background spans embedded systems at Lytup, backend architecture at OgaLandlord, and enterprise infrastructure at Coca-Cola HBC. Each role deepened my conviction that the best engineers understand the entire system, not just their slice of it.

Right now I'm focused on EkoFare — a civic tech platform solving real logistics problems in Lagos. It's the project that best captures how I think: start from first principles, design for fault tolerance, and build for the people who depend on it every day.
